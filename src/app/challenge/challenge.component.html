What is this page?

This is a page where you can challenge players from other clans If you are unable to see the "FIGHT ME" button, it means that the website was unable to find out client running on your computer.

If this is a mistake and the client is actually running, try either refreshing the web page, restarting your client or both. If you do not have the client you will not be able to challenge other users. Sorry!

To challenge another user just click the button next to their name. You will be taken to a lobby screen on your client. You will not be able to challenge another opponent until you refresh the page.

Anyway thats about all. Happy questing!
